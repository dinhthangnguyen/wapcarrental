Why this website

What is this?

This is fictional car sharing platform.

For who?

For everyone (Especial MIU Student)

We create a community to connect people who want to provide a car for rent and people who really need them for a couple of days or more.

Why need it?

Maybe we can help you a little.

Thereâ€™re many people here on campus without a car. When they want a car for a test drive or go to DOT they need to ask their friends or go search around for many places for the car. There is an existing issue that also an opportunity for a service to fill the gap.

The brief description of the project is a open platform for people to share their less utilized vehicles for rent at a decent price and reach customers in the area. They can just have a car, driver license, registration, and location of the car. the scope of this project as a small startup, we will do car sharing for Fairfield, Ottumwa, Mount Pleasant. then we can expand when we have more funding :)

How to use?

It is simple

On the user side, they go to the website, choose the city, select make, model, year, and last but not least the price. then book the car. Apply for the credit card, and then he can come any time to pick up the vehicle. The minimum rental is 1 day.
